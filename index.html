Welcome to TRC International University

TRC International University is a premier institution of higher learning dedicated to providing world-class education with a global perspective. Established in 2005, we have grown to become one of East Africa's most respected universities, with students from over 25 countries.

Our mission is to develop future leaders through innovative teaching, cutting-edge research, and a commitment to community service. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, all designed to meet international standards.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and partnerships with universities worldwide, TRC International University provides an enriching educational experience that prepares students for success in an increasingly interconnected world.

We are proud of our diverse student body and inclusive campus community that celebrates different cultures, perspectives, and ideas.

Registration Payment Instructions

Before submitting your application, you must complete the non-refundable registration fee payment of UGX 50,000 via mobile money.

  1. Go to your mobile money menu on your phone
  2. Select "Send Money" or "Pay Bill" option
  3. Enter the university secretary's number: +256 703959773
  4. Enter the amount: UGX 50,000
  5. Use your name as the reference
  6. Complete the transaction and save the transaction ID
  7. Enter the transaction ID in the form below
Pay to: +256 703959773
IMPORTANT: Your registration will only be processed after we verify your payment. The registration fee is NON-REFUNDABLE. After successful submission, a unique student registration number will be automatically sent to your email within 24-48 hours.
